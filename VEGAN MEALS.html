Chik'n Fajitas

20-25 min

Ingredients

  • Plant-based chicken
  • Red & yellow peppers
  • Refried pinto beans
  • Onions
  • Flour tortillas
  • Spices: cumin, cayenne, paprika, chili powder, garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Cut the red and yellow peppers into strips. Cook over stovetop on meadium to high heat to blacken slightly.
  2. Cut the onion into strips. Add to pan with peppers and begin and cook until golden brown. Add water as needed to prevent burning.
  3. Cook the chicken on medium heat for 3-5 minutes. Maintain soft texture.
  4. Heat beans in the microwave or over the stovetop for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Heat tortillas in the microwave for 15 seconds.
  6. Add ingredients to the tortilla, roll up, and garnish with cilantro, avocado, or salsa as desired.
  7. Enjoy!

Black Bean Chili

15-20 min.

Ingredients

  • Plant-based beef crumbles
  • 1 white onion
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 can crushed tomato
  • Spices: chili powder, cumin, cayenne, garlic powder, fennel, paprika, turmeric, black pepper

Instructions

  1. Dice the onion and cook in a large pot on medium to high heat until golden brown. Add water as needed.
  2. Cook the beef crumbles on low for 3-5 minutes. Season with spices to your liking.
  3. Add beans and crushed tomato to pot of onions. Bring to boil then reduce to medium heat.
  4. Add beef crumbles to the pot. Stir well.
  5. Season with spices. Heavy on chili powder and cumin. Light on all others.
  6. Serve with warm bread. Enjoy!

Spaghetti

25-30 min

Ingredients

  • Thin spaghetti
  • 1 large can peeled tomatoes
  • Plant-based beef crumbles
  • Fresh basil
  • 1 loaf Italian bread
  • Spices: oregano, basil, garlic powder, black pepper

Instructions

  1. Pour canned tomatoes into a large pan. Crush the tomatoes, then cover and bring to boil. Reduce to medium and leave covered, stirring occasionally.
  2. Cook beef crumbles in a pan over low heat for 3-5 minutes. Season as desired, then remove from heat and set aside.
  3. Add water to large pot and bring to boil. Add salt. Once boiling, add pasta and cook for 7-8 minutes. Drain when finished.
  4. Once tomatoes have thickened, uncover and add beef and spices.
  5. Wash fresh basil and chop into strips. Add to sauce. Reduce heat to low.
  6. Mix melted vegan butter and garlic powder. Pour over bread and bake for 10 minutes until crispy.
  7. Enjoy!
